SONIA DESIGN

SONIA DESIGN is an internationally recognized and award-winning interior and exterior design firm with expertise in residential, commercial, hospitality, retail, healthcare and beauty projects. Based avenue Hassan II, Malabo, Ecuatotial Guinea, SONIA DESIGN was established in 2005 by Paula Rosalia Bibang,who has become a prominent expert in the industry. The Design Group combines extraordinary talent and global experiences to create spaces, nationally and worldwide, that can be as “cutting edge” or “timeless” as the exceptionality of each of its Clients. Our Design Group’s mission is simple: develop interior and exterior design experiences that are distinctive, compelling and of super quality and individuality.
