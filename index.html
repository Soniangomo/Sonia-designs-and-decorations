About Us

SONIA DESIGN is an internationally recognized and award-winning interior and exterior design firm with expertise in residential, commercial, hospitality, retail, healthcare and beauty projects.

Based avenue Hassan II, Malabo, Ecuatotial Guinea, SONIA DESIGN was established in 2005 by Paula Rosalia Bibang,who has become a prominent expert in the industry.

The Design Group combines extraordinary talent and global experiences to create spaces, nationally and worldwide, that can be as “cutting edge” or “timeless” as the exceptionality of each of its Clients. Our Design Group’s mission is simple: develop interior and exterior design experiences that are distinctive, compelling and of super quality and individuality.

SONIA DESIGN AND DECORATIONS was create in october 10, 2005 by Paula Rosalia Bibang in a small workshop in Malabo. was a redeuce group with only 3 person. In 2015 they were sponsored by an international Chinese company "CHINA DALIAN S.L" for work with them and they are signed some contract with others International Chinese company in the same years In 2017 Paula Rosalia decided to make the company an international company partnering with different construction companies in whole the worl special Europe.
